What is included in the free trial?

The free trial gives you full access to everything included with Peacock Premium for 7 days. This includes our full content library of 20,000+ hours, live sports, next-day access to current seasons, and exclusive Peacock Originals.

How does the free trial work?

To start your free trial, simply sign up with your email and payment information. You'll have full access to Peacock Premium for 7 days at no charge. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically continue at the monthly rate of $5.99.

What happens after the free trial?

After your 7-day free trial ends, your subscription will automatically continue at the monthly rate of $5.99 plus applicable taxes. You can cancel at any time during your trial without being charged.

Can I cancel during the free trial?

Yes, you can cancel at any time during your free trial without being charged. If you cancel during the trial period, you'll still have access to Peacock Premium until the end of your 7-day trial period.

What devices support Peacock TV?

Peacock is supported on a wide range of devices including smartphones, tablets, computers, smart TVs (Samsung, LG, VIZIO), streaming devices (Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, Chromecast), and gaming consoles (Xbox, PlayStation).
